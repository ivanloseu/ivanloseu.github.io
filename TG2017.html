Conference "Transformation groups 2017"

Independent University of Moscow, Moscow, Russia, December 14-19, 2017

Topics of the conference:
Algebraic groups and Geometric Invariant theory.
Transformation groups in Differential geometry and Analysis.
Structure and representation theory of Lie algebras, Lie groups and their generalizations.
Discrete subgroups of Lie groups and discrete transformation groups.
