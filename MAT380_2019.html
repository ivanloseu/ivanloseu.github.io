MATH380 (Fall 2019): Modern Algebra I.

Instructor: Prof. Ivan Loseu (email: ivan.loseu@yale.edu)

Course web page: https://gauss.math.yale.edu/~il282/MAT380_2019

Lectures: TTh 1:00-2:15pm, LOM 205. The first day of class is 8/29, and the last day of class is 12/5.

Office hours: T 2.30-3.30pm, Th 10.30-11.30am in DL 416.

Goals: This course serves an introduction to Commutative and Homological algebra. We will be mostly following [AM] and [HS]. Additional references include [AK] and [E]. There is no required text book.

Grading: Based on 6 homework sets (60%) and a take-home final exam (40%).

Rules: For homework sets, peer discussion is allowed but only after your own thinking; you are also required to write your solution on your own, if it (partially) comes from a discussion. Homework sets and the take-home final should be submitted via Canvas. You are encouraged to texify your work, but scans of handwriting are also acceptable. Overdue submissions will not be accepted.

SYLLABUS:

  • 8/29, 8/31 --- Basics on rings and modules, examples

  • 9/5 --- Basics on rings and modules, examples

  • 9/10, 9/12 --- Category theory: categories, functors, and functor morphisms

  • 9/17, 9/19 --- Category theory: representable functors, adjoint functors

  • 9/24, 9/26 --- Category theory: additive functors between categories of modules, projective and injective modules.

  • 10/1, 10/3 --- Localization of rings and modules

  • 10/8, 10/10 --- Tensor products of modules

  • 10/15 --- Tensor products of modules

  • 10/22, 10/24 --- Tensor products vs rings

  • 10/29, 10/31 --- Chain conditions

  • 11/5, 11/7 --- Chain conditions

  • 11/12, 11/14 --- Basics on homological algebra: complexes, homology and homotopy

  • 11/26, 11/28 --- Basics on homological algebra: projective resolutions, Ext^1.

  • 12/3, 12/5 --- Basics on homological algebra: derived functors, injective resolutions.



References:
[AK] A. Altman, S. Kleiman, A Term of Commutative Algebra. Version of August 6, 2017, Worldwide Center of Mathematics, available here
[AM] M. Atiyah, I.G. Macdonald, Introduction to Commutative Algebra, Addison-Wesley, 1969.
[E] D. Eisenbud, Commutative Algebra: With a View Toward Algebraic Geometry, GTM 150, Springer-Verlag, 2004.
[HS] P.J. Hilton, U. Stammbach, A Course in Homological Algebra, 2nd edition. Springer-Verlag, 1997.
