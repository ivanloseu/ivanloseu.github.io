MATH380 (Fall 2019): Modern Algebra I.

Instructor: Prof. Ivan Loseu (email: ivan.loseu@yale.edu)

Course web page: https://gauss.math.yale.edu/~il282/MAT380_2019

Lectures: TTh 13:00-14:15, LOM 205. The first day of class is 8/29, and the last day of class is 12/5.

Office hours: T 2.30-3.30pm, Th 10.30-11.30 in DL 416.

Goals: This course serves an introduction to Commutative and Homological algebra. We will be mostly following [AM] and [HS]. Additional references include [AK] and [E]. There is no required text book.

Grading: Based on 6 homework sets (60%) and a take-home final exam (40%).

Rules: For homework sets, peer discussion is allowed but only after your own thinking; you are also required to write your solution on your own, if it (partially) comes from a discussion. Homework sets and the take-home final should be submitted in PDF form, with the file name 380HWn_[your last name].pdf to the instructor by email with the title line 380HWn (n=1,2,3,4,5,6). You are encouraged to texify your work, but scans of handwriting are also acceptable. Overdue submissions will not be accepted.

Final: The final exam will be take-home. It will be posted here on the evening of Dec 5, and will be due by the end of Dec 11. For the final, you are not allowed to discuss with anyone else; and you are not allowed to consult any external resources except your own notes and homework sets for this course.
