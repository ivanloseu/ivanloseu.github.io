Conference "Representation theory and Geometry of symplectic resolutions"

Northeastern University, Boston, May 18-22, 2015

The conference will concentrate on the representation theoretic and geometric questions connected to symplectic singularities and their resolutions. Related aspects of the geometric and categorical representation theory will also be discussed.

Organizers : Roman Bezrukavnikov, Pavel Etingof (MIT), Ivan Losev and Valerio Toledano Laredo (Northeastern)
