Preliminary list of topics:

  • Examples and classes of rings, ideals and modules.

  • Noetherian and Artinian rings and modules.

  • Principal ideal domains and their finitely generated modules.

  • Localization of rings and modules.

  • Introduction to Category theory.

  • Functors between categories of modules. Tensor products of modules and rings.

  • Integral extensions and closures.

  • Connections to Algebraic geometry.
